Description

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Serves: 2

British Classic! Fish and Chips!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes; peeled and cut
  • 2 Cod Fillets
  • Deep Fat Fryer and Sunflower Oil

Batter

  • 130g Plain Flour
  • 150ml Lager or Ale
  • 1 Teaspoon salt
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Powder

Mushy Peas Recipes

  • Frozen Peas (two handfuls)
  • Chopped Mint leaves (4 or 5)
  • Two Spring Onions chopped

Steps

  1. Begin by preparing the potatoes. Peel them and cut them into thick chips. Rinse them in cold water to remove any excess starch and pat them dry with paper towels.
  2. Heat the sunflower oil in a deep fat fryer or a large, deep saucepan until it reaches 180Â°C.
  3. While the oil is heating up, prepare the batter. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, lager or ale, salt, and baking powder until you have a smooth batter.
  4. Dip the cod fillets into the batter, making sure they are well coated on all sides.
  5. Once the oil has reached the correct temperature, carefully lower the battered cod fillets into the hot oil using a slotted spoon. Fry them for 6-8 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Remove them from the oil using the slotted spoon and place them on a wire rack to drain off any excess oil.
  6. While the fish is cooking, prepare the mushy peas. Cook the frozen peas according to the package instructions. Drain them and transfer them to a mixing bowl. Add the chopped mint leaves and spring onions, and use a fork to mash them together until you have a rough puree.
  7. Once the fish is cooked and drained, serve it hot with the chips and mushy peas on the side. Enjoy!

Notes

  • For an extra crispy batter, you can dust the cod fillets in a little bit of cornstarch before dipping them in the batter.
  • If you don't have a deep fat fryer, you can use a large, deep saucepan instead. Just make sure you don't fill it more than half full with oil, and be extra careful when lowering the fish into the hot oil.
  • You can also serve the fish and chips with some tartar sauce and lemon wedges for extra flavor.
