Description

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Serves: 2

English Breakfast

Ingredients

  • 4 Thick Sausages
  • 4 Slices of Bacon
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 Can of Baked Beans
  • 2 Large Tomatoes, halved
  • 4 Slices of Black Pudding
  • 4 Slices of Toasted Bread
  • Butter, as needed
  • Salt & Pepper, to taste
  • Olive Oil, as needed

Steps

  1. Begin by preheating the oven to 200Â°C. Line a baking tray with parchment paper and place the halved tomatoes on it, cut side up. Drizzle them with a little bit of olive oil and sprinkle them with salt and pepper. Roast them in the oven for 15-20 minutes, or until they are soft and slightly caramelized.
  2. While the tomatoes are roasting, cook the sausages and bacon. Heat a large frying pan over medium-high heat and add a little bit of olive oil. Once the oil is hot, add the sausages and bacon and fry them for 10-12 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Transfer them to a plate lined with paper towels to drain off any excess oil.
  3. In the same pan, cook the black pudding slices for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are crispy and cooked through. Remove them from the pan and keep them warm.
  4. In a separate saucepan, heat the baked beans over low heat, stirring occasionally.
  5. In a non-stick frying pan, melt some butter over medium heat. Crack the eggs into the pan and fry them to your liking. You can either cook them sunny-side up, over-easy, or scrambled.
  6. Once everything is cooked, arrange the sausages, bacon, black pudding, and roasted tomatoes on two plates. Serve the baked beans and toasted bread on the side. Top the eggs with a sprinkle of salt and pepper and place them on the plates. Serve hot and enjoy!

Notes

  • You can use any type of sausages and bacon you like, but traditionally, English breakfast is made with pork sausages and back bacon.
  • You can also add some grilled mushrooms or fried potatoes to the breakfast if you like.
  • Don't forget to brew a cup of tea or coffee to complete the perfect English breakfast experience!
